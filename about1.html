About Us

ShambaSalama is a smart pest deterrent system designed to effectively scare away quelea birds and locusts, protecting your crops and ensuring a bountiful harvest. Utilizing advanced technology, our system operates efficiently to safeguard your fields, minimizing damage without harming the environment. With ShambaSalama, farmers can achieve optimal crop yields while maintaining eco-friendly practices.
